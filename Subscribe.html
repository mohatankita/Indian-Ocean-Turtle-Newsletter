SUBSCRIBE

The Indian Ocean Turtle Newsletter is published twice a year and is provided free of cost to all subscribers. However, we encourage online usage of the newsletter. The hard copy is primarily intended to increase readership in the region, especially among target groups with poor internet access. If you wish to subscribe to the hard copy issues, please consider making a donation to help support printing and distribution costs. IOTN is published biannually, in January and July.
